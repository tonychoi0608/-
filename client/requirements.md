## Notes
- The application uses a warm, family-friendly color palette (soft terracotta/coral and warm off-white) to match the "Hyodo Calendar" theme.
- Display font: 'Outfit' (clean, geometric, friendly).
- Body font: 'DM Sans' (highly readable, modern).
- Feedbacks are sorted newest first on the client side to ensure testers always see the latest inputs at the top.
